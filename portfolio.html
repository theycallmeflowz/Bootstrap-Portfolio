Geo Trending News

Geo Trending News Website was developed with HTML5, CSS3, Javascript & JQuery. It lets users get news articles from anywhere in the world by clicking on a desired country on the map. It uses the Google Maps, Google News and Bing News API, to display information. User Accounts are authenticated with Firebase.

res.Create

res.Create was developed with NodeJs, Express Js, Handlebars, MySQL & Sequelize. Users can create their resume on this website after entering all required information. Users get 3 different formatting styles and can print out their resume. User Accounts are authenticated with npm packages, Passport js & bcrypt.

LibiQ

Libiq helps users track books they get from the library to help make sure they return it on time. Libiq was designed with React Js, Express, Node, & MongoDB on the backend.
